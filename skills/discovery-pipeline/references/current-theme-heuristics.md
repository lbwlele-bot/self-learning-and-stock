# Current Theme Heuristics

Use these when the active topic is around:

- high-friction world
- dollar weaponization
- A-share discount and local absorption
- retail niche

## Prefer

- internal security and resilience spending
- substitute chains with visible customer validation
- budget-backed or policy-backed links that can enter the profit statement
- second-line or under-modeled key links

## Demote

- the most crowded total leaders
- headline-only beneficiaries
- symbolic national champions without a clear profit path
- names where the user is mostly doing crowd homework

## Required output

- candidate chain
- why the link matters
- why the market may still not have fully seen it
- what needs to be validated next
